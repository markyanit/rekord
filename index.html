THE SOUNDTRACK OF YOUR LIFE

Rekord is a platform designed to foster connections with friends through the shared love of music. Our mission is to provide an outlet for anyone to express themselves by using the universal art of music as a bridge between people's inner thoughts and the rest of the world. Here on Rekord, music is used to represent the moments that happen in our lives. Whether they are the gloomy moments after a breakup or the exciting moments of finally landing that dream job, there is always a song to play that embodies the essence of these moments. When put together, they become the soundtrack of your life.

DISCOVER NEW MUSIC

You can learn a lot about a person by what they listen to or who their favorite artists are. With Rekord, you can display the song that you're currently playing for your friends to see in real time. You can find out what your friends are listening to as well and discover how impressive (or weird) their music taste is. Just simply connect your Rekord account with your preferred music streaming platform and the songs you stream will automatically be displayed on Rekord. Bet you didn't know that your timid friend Harry loves rocking out to Taylor Swift every morning!

Connect to any of these music streaming platforms.

SHARE YOUR MOMENTS THROUGH SONG

Just like in a musical, there is always a song to go along with each scene. When you share your thoughts, feelings, or the events that happen in your life through text posts on Rekord, you also select a song that best represents these moments. Sometimes words are not enough to fully convey whatever's going on in your life, so selecting the perfect song that plays in the background can be more compelling when sharing these moments on Rekord. We just hope that they're not always sappy love songs!

BUILD YOUR SOUNDTRACK

Your Rekord profile compiles and displays all your posts into a list, which becomes the soundtrack of your life! In your profile, you can control your music display, customize your biography, message your followers, and look back on all the previous tracks you've posted. You could also create customized playlists for your friends to listen to on any music streaming platform. Need a really hyped up playlist to listen during your HIIT workout? Kelly's Gym Sesh playlist on Rekord just might be the perfect one!
